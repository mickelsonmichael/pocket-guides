Leveling Up

Maximum level is 5. You gain levels by fighting. On levelup, you receive 5 points to allocate to one stat.

StatEffect
HPIncreases survivability in battle.
StrengthIncreases damage from damage cards and command cards.
KiIncreases damage from beam attacks.
SpeedImproves accuracy and avoidance. Also determines who attacks first.

Battle System

Overview

Battles are card-based. Each turn you choose from the command bar at the bottom of the screen. HP of both fighters is shown in opposite corners — reduce the enemy to zero to win.

If a battle allows 2 fighters, use C (Select Character) to swap them. If one dies, the other is brought out automatically.

Commands

CommandDescription
B — BasicAttacking: Gather power, or perform a 3-stage attack (4-stage if powered up).
Defending: Move or defend. Defending reduces incoming damage; moving changes your position (see Position table).
J — JointUse support and ability cards from your deck. May or may not cost CC depending on the card.
L — LimitedCards usable repeatedly if you have enough CC. Characters can hold up to 3 Limited cards (Androids 19 & 20 can only hold 2). To assign limited cards: Menu → Team → select character → "Limit".
C — CharacterSwitch to your other fighter if one is available.

Position Effects

PositionVulnerable ToFrontBack
In the AirBeam AttacksAttack ↑, Defense ↓Attack ↓, Defense ↑
On the GroundDamage AttacksAttack ↑, Defense ↓Attack ↓, Defense ↑

Basic attacks do not cost CC.

Card Cost (CC)

CC is shown in the lower-right corner of the battle screen as CC: /13. You need CC to use Limited cards. Ways to gain CC:

TriggerCC Gained
You are the attacker+3
Each successful command in a Command Card (3-stage = 3 commands)+1 per command
Your HP drops substantially+2
Hit in the air by Beam, or on the ground by Damage (amount varies by position)Variable

Enemy AI Pattern

Regular attack → Gather power → 5-stage attack → Beam or Damage attack → Repeat. Enemies rarely use energy cards unless powered up — so don't waste yours either unless necessary.

Post-Battle

After each battle, you choose 1 card from 3 options.

Walkthrough & Boss Guide

SAIYAN SAGA

The game starts after Raditz's defeat. Piccolo is training Gohan. Talk to Piccolo, find the jars (answer the battle system question to get a card), return to Piccolo and repeat until he asks you to pick a deck. Recommended: Beam Deck.

PiccoloEASY

When he uses Special Beam Cannon, use an avoid card or move Gohan to the bottom-left corner.

After scene: the Saiyans arrive and Piccolo dies saving Gohan. Fight Nappa as Gohan → pick a card.

NappaNORMAL

Goku arrives and leads Vegeta away. Before the Vegeta fight, equip Goku's limit with Super Kamehameha and Kaioken.

Vegeta (Goku)HARD (first time)

When he powers up: Kaioken on defense, Super Kamehameha on offense. Watch for Galick-Ho.

After Vegeta beats Goku: go to the nearest rock formation and press A to get Yajirobe's sword. Talk to Krillin → say yes. Talk to Yajirobe.

Vegeta (Gohan/Krillin)NORMAL

No Kaioken or Super Kamehameha available.

NAMEK SAGA

On the map, go left to the Namekian village. Find the house reachable only through the row of trees → press A → get the Dende card. Go to the island and press A by the rightmost tree to dig up a Dragon Ball. A Namekian accuses you; dodge left.

GuldoEASY

Fight with Krillin here to start leveling him up.

RecoomeALMOST HARD

Uses Eraser Gun after taking significant damage.

Jeice & BurterNORMAL

Put Gohan front, Goku back. Use Dende near ¼ HP. After Jeice falls, Burter switches to Mach Attack — have Shockwave ready.

After Burter/Jeice: go to the top-left corner of the map and press A at the space pod for a card. Follow the dirt path north → collect Dragon Balls → talk to Krillin.

Ginyu (Goku's body) & JeiceEASY

Ginyu uses Super Kamehameha. Jeice may use Crasher Ball.

Freeza (1st fight)ALMOST HARD

Favors Ground Wave and Super Dash Elbow after powering up.

After the first Freeza fight: Freeza nearly kills Piccolo and kills Krillin, pushing Goku to go Super Saiyan. Switch to Gohan, find Bulma in the NW of the map. Talk to her → say no → go to the pile of rocks and press A for a card → go back to Bulma → say yes.

Freeza (2nd fight — SSJ Goku)ALMOST HARD

No Kaioken available.

ANDROID SAGA

After Namek, talk to Piccolo and search for him 5 times (find his ki). Get Gohan's Dunk Shot from Piccolo, a card from Chichi, and the 5 Stage Attack from Goku.

Goku (training)EASY

Uses Spirit Bomb when firing Ki attacks.

After scene: talk to Krillin, Vegeta, and Piccolo. Find Tien in the northern cliffs (press A).

Android 19NORMAL

Can absorb Ki attacks. Has an HP drain move.

Android 20NORMAL

Uses Super E-Ball (19 does not).

Android 17 (Piccolo-God)SEMI-NORMAL

Won't absorb energy. Uses Crazy Rush and Super E-Ball — have Special Beam Cannon ready.

Cell (first encounter)NORMAL

Lose intentionally to unlock Piccolo-God, then continue. Watch for Special Beam Cannon and Super Kamehameha.

After Cell escapes: in the chamber building, find the note behind the building and follow its instructions to get Kaio, Trunks' Sword, and Solid Shot. Talk to Goku for something strange.

Android 16 & 18ALMOST HARD

16 favors Rocket Punch. Have Goku-SS assist if possible.

CELL GAMES
Cell (vs Goku)NORMAL

Now uses Cell Combo. Favors Special Beam Cannon.

Cell Jr. (vs Gohan SSJ2)VERY EASY
Cell (vs Gohan)NORMAL

Gohan has Super Kamehameha — use it.

Cell (vs Vegeta — FINAL)NORMAL

Use Vegeta here to unlock the extra Trunks story.

TRUNKS' STORY (unlockable)

Trunks returns to his future. Talk to NPCs on the map for character unlock hints. Use Super Trunks — include the card received from Bulma in your deck.

Androids 17 & 18NORMAL
CellNORMAL
BUU SAGA

6 years later. Gohan is in high school. On the map: Piccolo is in the NW corner, Videl in SE. Talk to Goten and fight him (easy training bout). Then fight Young Trunks at the Martial Arts Tournament (Goten's perspective).

Young Trunks (kids tournament)NORMAL

Uses Burning Attack and Flash Punch. Have Goten's Kamekameha ready.

After the tournament: talk to everyone on the Babidi map to advance.

Majin Vegeta (vs SSJ2 Goku)NORMAL

Has Final Flash and Big Bang Attack. Flies up when near death — Spirit Bomb.

On the next map: press A on Piccolo and Krillin → find Videl → talk to Trunks.

Fat BuuNORMAL

Watch for Super E-Ball and Potbelly Attack.

Goten & Trunks master fusion. Gohan gets powered up by Old Kai.

Young Trunks (Time Chamber)NORMAL

Uses Rising Kick and Burning Attack.

Buu (vs Gotenks SSJ3)NORMAL

Watch for Super E-Ball.

Buu (vs Mystic Gohan)NORMAL
Buu (Gotenks + Piccolo absorbed)NORMAL

Counters Super Kamehameha with Breathe — avoid it and use other cards instead.

Buu (vs Vegito)NORMAL

Load Vegito with Super Kamehameha, Teleportation, and Big Bang Attack.

Evil Buu (Final Boss)NORMAL+

Have SSJ3 Goku unlocked — it makes this significantly easier.
