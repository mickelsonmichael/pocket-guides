Leveling Up

Maximum level is 5. You gain levels by fighting. On levelup, you receive 5 points to allocate to one stat.

Battle System

Overview

Battles are card-based. Each turn you choose from the command bar at the bottom of the screen. HP of both fighters is shown in opposite corners — reduce the enemy to zero to win.

If a battle allows 2 fighters, use C (Select Character) to swap them. If one dies, the other is brought out automatically.

Commands

CommandDescription
B — BasicAttacking: Gather power, or perform a 3-stage attack (4-stage if powered up).
Defending: Move or defend. Defending reduces incoming damage; moving changes your position (see Position table).
J — JointUse support and ability cards from your deck. May or may not cost CC depending on the card.
L — LimitedCards usable repeatedly if you have enough CC. Characters can hold up to 3 Limited cards (Androids 19 & 20 can only hold 2). To assign limited cards: Menu → Team → select character → "Limit".
C — CharacterSwitch to your other fighter if one is available.

Position Effects

PositionVulnerable ToFrontBack
In the AirBeam AttacksAttack ↑, Defense ↓Attack ↓, Defense ↑
On the GroundDamage AttacksAttack ↑, Defense ↓Attack ↓, Defense ↑

Basic attacks do not cost CC.

Card Cost (CC)

CC is shown in the lower-right corner of the battle screen as CC: /13. You need CC to use Limited cards. Ways to gain CC:

TriggerCC Gained
You are the attacker+3
Each successful command in a Command Card (3-stage = 3 commands)+1 per command
Your HP drops substantially+2
Hit in the air by Beam, or on the ground by Damage (amount varies by position)Variable

Enemy AI Pattern

Regular attack → Gather power → 5-stage attack → Beam or Damage attack → Repeat. Enemies rarely use energy cards unless powered up — so don't waste yours either unless necessary.

Post-Battle

After each battle, you choose 1 card from 3 options.

Walkthrough

SAIYAN SAGA

The game starts after Raditz's defeat. Piccolo is training Gohan. Talk to Piccolo, find the jars (answer the battle system question to get a card), return to Piccolo and repeat until he asks you to pick a deck. Recommended: Beam Deck.

Fight Piccolo → Pick a card → Watch scene.

After the Saiyans arrive and Piccolo dies saving Gohan: fight Nappa as Gohan → Pick a card.

Goku arrives and leads Vegeta away. Before the Vegeta fight, equip Goku's limit with Super Kamehameha and Kaioken.

Fight Vegeta (Goku) → Pick card → scene.

After Vegeta beats Goku: Go to the nearest rock formation and press A to get Yajirobe's sword. Talk to Krillin → say yes. Talk to Yajirobe. Fight Vegeta again (Gohan/Krillin) → pick card.

NAMEK SAGA

On the map, go left to the Namekian village. Find the house reachable only through the row of trees → press A → get the Dende card. Go to the island and press A by the rightmost tree to dig up a Dragon Ball. A Namekian accuses you; dodge left.

Fight Guldo → fight Recoome → fight Jeice & Burter.

After Burter/Jeice: go to the top-left corner of the map and press A at the space pod for a card. Follow the dirt path north → collect Dragon Balls → talk to Krillin.

Fight Ginyu (Goku's body) & Jeice → fight Freeza.

After first Freeza fight: Freeza nearly kills Piccolo and kills Krillin, pushing Goku to go Super Saiyan. Switch to Gohan, find Bulma in the NW of the map. Talk to her → say no → go to the pile of rocks and press A for a card → go back to Bulma → say yes. Fight Freeza again (as SSJ Goku).

ANDROID SAGA

After Namek, talk to Piccolo and search for him 5 times (find his ki). Get Gohan's Dunk Shot from Piccolo, another card from Chichi, and the 5 Stage Attack from Goku. Fight Goku (training battle).

After scene: Talk to Krillin, Vegeta, and Piccolo. Find Tien in the northern cliffs (press A). Fight Android 20 → fight Cell (lose once to unlock Piccolo-God, then win).

After Cell escapes: In the chamber building, find the note behind the building and follow its instructions to get Kaio, Trunks' Sword, and Solid Shot. Talk to Goku for something strange. Fight Android 16 & 18.

CELL GAMES

Cell Games begin. Fight Cell as Goku → then as SSJ2 Gohan (Cell Jr. battle) → Gohan vs Cell main fight → Use Vegeta for the final Cell battle to unlock the extra Trunks story.

TRUNKS' STORY (unlockable)

Trunks returns to his future. Talk to NPCs on the map for character unlock hints. Fight Androids 17 & 18 as Super Trunks (use the card from Bulma). Fight Cell.

BUU SAGA

6 years later. Gohan is in high school. On the map: Piccolo is in the NW corner, Videl in SE. Talk to Goten and fight him (easy). Then fight Young Trunks at the Martial Arts Tournament (Goten's perspective).

After the tournament: Talk to everyone on the Babidi map to advance. Fight Majin Vegeta as SSJ2 Goku.

On the next map: Press A on Piccolo and Krillin → find Videl → talk to Trunks. Fight Fat Buu.

Goten & Trunks master fusion. Gohan gets powered up by Old Kai. Fight Buu (normal) → Buu (absorb) → Buu (Gotenks absorbed) → Buu as Vegito → Evil Buu (final boss). Recommended: have SSJ3 Goku unlocked for the final fight.

Boss Guide

Walkthrough bosses only — bosses that appear exclusively in the walkthrough are listed with full tips here. Second-round unlock bosses follow the same general strategy: build CC, power up, use your best attack.

Piccolo EASY

You attack first. Use a 3-stage attack for CC. If he gathers power, do the same. Use Foresight before his 4/5-stage attack if you have it. Try a 4-stage attack or Masenko. When he uses Special Beam Cannon, use an avoid card or move Gohan to the bottom-left corner. Repeat.

Nappa NORMAL

Same strategy as Piccolo. Pummel with 3- and 4-stage attacks. If he hasn't powered up by his 4th or 5th turn, have Endurance ready. If he does power up, use the Piccolo strategy.

Vegeta (Goku) HARD (first time)

Before the fight, set Goku's limits to Super Kamehameha and Kaioken. Attack normally with 3- and 4-stage attacks. When Vegeta powers up, have Goku power up too, use Kaioken on defense, then Super Kamehameha on offense. Watch out for Galick-Ho at this stage — it hurts.

Vegeta (Gohan/Krillin) NORMAL

Same as the Goku fight, but without Goku's support. Same strategy applies.

Guldo EASY

Choose Krillin (to get him to at least level 2) or Gohan. Standard strategy throughout.

Recoome ALMOST HARD

Wail with 3- and 4-stage attacks. After significant damage, he'll use Eraser Gun for heavy damage — be ready for it.

Jeice & Burter NORMAL

First 2v2 battle. Put Gohan front, Goku back. Use Dende when either is near 1/4 HP. Once Jeice is down, Burter switches to Mach Attack — have Shockwave ready. When possible, have Goku power up → Kaioken → Spirit Bomb.

Ginyu (Goku's body) & Jeice EASY

Ginyu will use Super Kamehameha — be careful. Standard tactics. Swap to Krillin if Gohan gets low; use Misty Ki Wave with Krillin. Jeice may or may not use Crasher Ball. Swap back to Gohan if Krillin gets low and finish Jeice.

Freeza (1st fight) ALMOST HARD

Freeza loves Ground Wave and Super Dash Elbow after powering up. Build CC to ~65–70, then use Kaioken + Spirit Bomb for massive damage.

Freeza (2nd fight — SSJ Goku) ALMOST HARD

Same as before but no Kaioken. Spirit Bomb still works. Build CC with normal tactics, then spam Super Kamehameha and Spirit Bombs.

Goku (training) EASY

Pushover. But he will use Spirit Bomb when he fires Ki attacks, which drains HP fast. Build CC and use Masenkos or Dunk Shot.

Android 19 NORMAL

May or may not absorb Ki attacks (doesn't do it often). Has an HP drain attack. Build CC, power up, Spirit Bomb.

Android 20 NORMAL

Same as 19, but uses Super E-Ball as his special. Normal strategy.

Cell (first encounter) NORMAL

Have Piccolo-God if possible. If not, lose once → continue → beat him. Watch for Special Beam Cannon and Super Kamehameha.

Android 17 SEMI-NORMAL

Piccolo-God makes this much easier. Won't absorb energy but uses Crazy Rush and Super E-Ball. Normal tactics; use Special Beam Cannon when available.

Android 16 & 18 ALMOST HARD

16 favors Rocket Punch; 18 uses a variety. Have Goku-SS assist if possible.

Cell (Cell Games — vs Goku) NORMAL

Same as first encounter but may use Cell Combo. He mainly uses Special Beam Cannon.

Cell Jr. (Gohan SSJ2) VERY EASY

Same Cell strategy. Easy.

Cell (vs Gohan) NORMAL

Gohan can now use Super Kamehameha. Set it up and use the same Cell strategy.

Cell (vs Vegeta — FINAL) NORMAL

Same strategy. Use Vegeta here to unlock the extra Trunks story.

Androids 17 & 18 (Trunks' story) NORMAL

Use Super Trunks only. Include the card received from Bulma in your deck.

Cell (Trunks' story) NORMAL

Same strategies used when fighting him as Piccolo.

Trunks (kids tournament) NORMAL

Stronger than Goten. Uses Burning Attack and Flash Punch. Have Goten's Kamekameha. Guard specials while using normal strategy.

Majin Vegeta NORMAL

You have SSJ2 Goku. Vegeta now has Final Flash and Big Bang Attack. Build CC and power without dealing too much damage. If he flies up: Spirit Bomb.

Fat Buu NORMAL

Watch out for Super E-Ball and Potbelly Attack. Normal strategy. Final Flash or Big Bang Attack helps.

Young Trunks (Time Chamber) NORMAL

Uses Rising Kick and Burning Attack. Normal tactics.

Buu (vs Gotenks SSJ3) NORMAL

Watch for Super E-Ball. Normal tactics.

Buu (vs Mystic Gohan) NORMAL

Same as above. Use Super Kamehameha if Gohan has it.

Buu (Gotenks + Piccolo absorbed) NORMAL

This Buu counters Super Kamehameha with Breathe. Avoid it; use any other cards while sticking to normal strategy to learn his moves.

Buu (vs Vegito) NORMAL

Equip Vegito with Super Kamehameha, Teleportation, and Big Bang Attack. Should be straightforward.

Evil Buu (Final Boss) NORMAL+

Have SSJ3 Goku unlocked — it makes this significantly easier. Standard strategy by this point.
